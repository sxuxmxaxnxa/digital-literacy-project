# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone
- Always verify UPI payment requests before approving them
- Avoid scanning unknown QR codes or accepting unexpected payment requests
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Do not click on suspicious links or messages from unknown sources
- Regularly update your apps and devices to stay protected from security threats
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Be cautious while sharing personal information on social media
- In case of fraud, report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number 1930
