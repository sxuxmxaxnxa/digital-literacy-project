# Social Media Etiquette Checklist

## Do’s
- Use respectful and polite language
- Maintain a professional tone when required
- Verify information before sharing
- Respect others’ opinions and privacy
- Think before posting content

## Don’ts
- Avoid using offensive or inappropriate language
- Do not spread false information
- Avoid oversharing personal details
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or arguments
- Avoid posting content that can harm your reputation
